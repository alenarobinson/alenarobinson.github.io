Introduction

InTents

I am going to create a digital magazine called InTents. It is going to be a travel blog style that shares all things camping, backpacking, and outdoor living. There will be location recommendations, lists of what to do in those locations, meal plans, packing lists, and more. I want it to be fairly minimalist and modern, but still have a hand-crafted artisan feel. I will achieve this through the use of muted, neutral/earthy tones, pairing a simple modern typeface with a more handwritten typeface, and using mostly film style photography.
